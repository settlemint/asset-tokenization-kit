# The $2.3 Trillion Opportunity

**Real-world assets represent $230 trillion in value trapped in analog systems. The tokenization market stalled at $50 billion because institutions cannot trust fragmented infrastructure.**

## Market Reality

<!-- SOURCE: Part I/Chapter 1 — The System Is Broken.md, lines 1-3 -->

The tokenization market stalled because it runs on a patchwork of point solutions, bolt-on compliance, and legacy settlement rails. Issuers juggle five vendors to get one asset live. Compliance lives outside the asset. Custody is an afterthought. Secondary trading exists in silos. Cash and digital securities never settle together. The result is risk, latency, reconciliation churn, and low trust, exactly what institutions won't accept.

The opportunity is both real and urgent. Real-world assets (RWAs) passed $50B on-chain in 2024 and are projected to hit $500B+ in 2025, excluding stablecoins. Yet this represents a fraction of a $230T addressable base. Institutional intent is there, and regulatory clarity is improving with frameworks like MiCA. But the current technology stack blocks scale.

### Why Point Tools Fail

**Issuance-only platforms**: Fast to mint, dead after T+0. No venue APIs, no DvP, no corporate actions - just reconciliation hell.

**Custody-led platforms**: Strong keys, weak market connectivity. Don't solve eligibility, venue integration, or corporate actions.

**Exchange-led platforms**: Order books without issuer truth. No compliance gates, no cap table sync, constant reconciliation gaps.

**Compliance middleware**: Runs checks on the side, not in state transitions where they matter. Detects violations instead of preventing them.

**Protocol-only standards**: Stop at transfer gating, leave everything else to build. Months of integration to recreate table stakes.
