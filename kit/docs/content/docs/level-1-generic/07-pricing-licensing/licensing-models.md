# Licensing Models

| Component | Licence | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Smart contracts, charts, kit utilities | **FSL-1.1-MIT** | Source-available with MIT grant for designated parts |
| Backend services (Transaction Signer, Portal/API, runtime services) | **Proprietary SettleMint licence** | Required for self-hosted deployments |
| SaaS subscription | **Included in subscription** | Backend licence bundled with managed service |

**Renewals & pricing:** Commercial figures—including pricing, volume discounts, and renewal terms—require direct engagement with SettleMint Sales. No numbers are published in the approved sources, so these remain **[PENDING SALES APPROVAL]**.
